Investing Tips

Read through our collection of investing tips written by experts!

Books for Beginning Investors:

  • The Little Book That Still Beats the Market by Joel Greenblatt
  • Common Sense on Mutual Funds by John C. Bogle
  • The Psychology of Money: Timeless Lessons on Wealth, Greed, and Happiness by Morgan Housel

    • General Investing Tips:

  • Don’t invest in money you’ll need soon...it’s too risky since there’s a chance you’ll lose money by selling stocks for a price less than what you bought them for.
  • Look at how much money you’re allotting for investments and be realistic. Remember the risks and ensure that you’re setting aside enough money to pay for daily expenses and important bills.
  • Have a diversified portfolio. The market is unpredictable and shifts all the time, and with diverse investments, you’ll have some stocks that are rising while others are falling.
  • To build on the previous point, invest in overseas markets as well! They add diversity to your portfolio and may offer higher returns as well.
  • Make sure you’re updated and informed on the markets. You can do this by reading articles from online news sites, signing up for Google Alerts, listening to various podcasts, and more.
